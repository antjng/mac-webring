MAC CS WEBRING
What's a webring? How can I join?

This a webring (i.e., a circular list of links) consisting of personal websites made by students of computer science (or math, or engineering, or anything else) at McMaster University.

Whether the loop is symbolic or merely convenient has not been determined. Early evidence suggests convenience.

If you are affiliated with McMaster and would like your site included, you can do so by opening a pull request, which is reviewed by an actual human, usually within a reasonable amount of time.*

*“Reasonable” is defined contextually.
